Frequently Asked Question

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

All standard orders around Manila will be shipped within the day while outside Metro Manila will be shipped 4 to 5 business days of confirmation from our team that they have received your order. If your order includes an item that we indicated will be shipped at a later date, we will only ship the entire order out together once the item arrives.

How do I place a return or exchange?

It’s our goal to ensure you have the best possible experience with us, and so we offer returns valid for 30 days from the date of arrival. As such, you will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. To return your item, do email/contact us at (tondolokalshop@gmail.com / 63+ 994 939 2833)

If 15 days have gone by since you’ve received the parcel, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Gift cards are non-returnable. Please note that shipping cost is not refunded.

How do I know which size fits me?

We provide a size chart (INCHES/CM) and a size guide at the end of the product image gallery. If you have any specific questions please feel to email us at tondolokalshop@gmail.com

How can I ensure that the parcel you’re shipping is well-packed?

To ensure that the parcel we're shipping is well-packed, we use adhesive plastic packaging and eco-friendly bags. If it's for same-day delivery or walk-in. Same with parcel delivery, we'll add bubble wrap and a pouch.
