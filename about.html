About Us

Our History

The Tondo Lokal Clothing Shop opened its doors in August 2021 and comprises three merged brands: ViceVersa Apparel (VVA), Money Drudgery Clothing (MDC), and Brand New With Tag Clothing (BNWT). The owners of this business are friends who share a passion for editing and design, particularly adoring clothing that embodies fashion. They opened their store in a vape shop because one of the owners had a vape shop. There used to be a clothes store, and when they hung out there, they came up with the concept of co-clothing. Each of the three pals has unique capabilities; one is famous and influential in Tondo Manila, one is skilled at designing or editing, and the last one has a shop.

The three friends pushed to expand their business and dreamed of becoming the top clothing seller in Tondo Manila since they knew a wide range of circles of friends who would support them. This gave them confidence in doing business.

Our Mission

to provide elegant and sophisticated formal wear that celebrates Filipino craftsmanship and style. We are dedicated to showcasing the rich heritage of Filipino textiles and design, offering impeccably tailored pieces that empower individuals to make a statement with confidence and grace on every occasion.

Our Vision

to become the go-to destination for locally crafted clothing that reflects the vibrant spirit and culture of our community. We aim to celebrate Filipino craftsmanship while promoting sustainable and ethical fashion practices. Through our curated selection of apparel, we aspire to empower local artisans and contribute to the growth of our community's economy
